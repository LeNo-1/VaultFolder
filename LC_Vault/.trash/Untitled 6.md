 
- 15 teaspoons Nutella ((heaped))  

Scoop heaped teaspoons of Nutella onto a lined tray, and freeze until solid. This usually takes 30-60 minutes Once the Nutella has frozen, make the cookie dough 

---
- 115 g unsalted butter  
- 175 g light brown sugar  
- 1 tsp vanilla  
- 1 medium egg  
- 275 g plain flour 

Beat together the butter and sugar until fluffy, and then add in the vanilla and egg and beat again until smooth.
