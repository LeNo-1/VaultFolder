## Ingredients
- **15 tsp** Nutella
- **115 g** unsalted butter
- **175 g** light brown sugar
- **1 tsp** vanilla
- **1 medium** egg
- **275 g** all-purpose flour
- **0.5 tsp** salt
- **0.5 tsp** bicarbonate of soda
- **1 tbsp** cornflour
- **200 g** finely chopped chocolate

## Steps
1. \>\>\> Roisin's Cookies
2. Scoop heaped teaspoons of **Nutella** onto a lined tray, and freeze until solid. This usually takes 30 to **60 minutes**
3. Once the Nutella has frozen, make the cookie dough
4. Preheat the oven to 190C/170C Fan 
5. Beat together the **unsalted butter** and **light brown sugar** until fluffy, and then add in the **vanilla** and **egg** and beat again until smooth.
6. Whisk together the **all-purpose flour**, **salt**, **bicarbonate of soda** and **cornflour**, and then add in to the other mix.
7. Add in the **finely chopped chocolate** and mix until combined.
8. Scoop the cookies with a 5cm cookie scoop to portion (or split into 15)
9. As you scoop each cookie, flatten the dough slightly into a disc shape, and add a frozen lump of Nutella to the middle and then wrap the cookie dough around the Nutella fully so its covered They will be balls of cookie in the end 
10. Line 2-3 LARGE trays with parchment paper \- and add 6 cookies to each tray. Each cookie might spread a bit whilst baking, so you want room for them to grow\!
11. Bake in the oven for 11 to **13 minutes**, or until golden. The more you bake, the less gooey they will be\!
